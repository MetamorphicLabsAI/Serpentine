# SERPENTINE: Character Encyclopedia

> *"In the endless void of the digital frontier, 49 distinct entities await. Each carries its own story, its own power, its own path to mastery."*

---

## Introduction to the Character System

Serpentine features a diverse roster of **49 playable characters**, each with unique visual aesthetics, unlock methods, and special mechanics. Characters range from the five default protocols available at startup to legendary entities unlocked through extraordinary feats of skill, dedication, or digital sorcery.

### Character Properties

Every character possesses:

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **ID** | Unique identifier used in game files and code |
| **Name** | Display name shown in menus and during gameplay |
| **Color** | Primary color scheme (hex codes or special effects) |
| **Unlock Method** | How the character becomes available |
| **Special Mechanic** | Unique gameplay or visual effect |

### Character Categories

Characters are organized by their unlock methodology:

- **Default Characters** — Available immediately upon first launch
- **Shop Characters** — Purchased with hard-earned PTS currency
- **Milestone Characters** — Unlocked through gameplay achievements
- **Speedrun Characters** — Rewarding lightning-fast completion times
- **Challenge Characters** — Earned through mastery of specific game modes
- **Secret Characters** — Hidden behind Easter eggs and unconventional methods
- **Seasonal Characters** — Limited-time availability tied to calendar events
- **Variant Characters** — Evolved forms of default characters

## Default Characters

These five entities form the foundation of Serpentine. They are available immediately—no unlocking required.

### Lore: The Core Five

> **NEON PROTOCOL** — Born from the first lines of code that sparked Serpentine into existence. Its cyan glow represents pure digital energy, the fundamental force underlying all protocols.

> **VOID WALKER** — Emerged from the spaces between data streams. Where others see emptiness, Void Walker perceives infinite possibility. Its purple essence drifts through dimensions unknown.

> **GOLD-FI** — The merchant class of the digital realm. It hoards power-points with obsessive precision, believing wealth to be the ultimate measure of worth.

> **GLITCH-WAVE** — A corrupted entity, forever glitching between states of being. Some say it was once a perfect protocol before a catastrophic system error fractured its essence.

> **MECHA-SERPENT** — The android of the group—cold, calculating, efficient. Built in ancient robot factories, it follows logic where others follow instinct.

## Secret Characters

Hidden behind Easter eggs, unconventional inputs, or statistical curiosities. These characters reward curiosity and experimentation.

> **9093 — The Forbidden Sequence**
> Legend speaks of a sequence: hold the 9 key for 9 seconds. Those who perform this ritual reportedly unlock an entity that exists outside normal game rules—a 9-segment anomaly that defies the laws of serpent physics.

> **MISSINGNO — The Glitch Entity**
> A corrupted fragment of data, accidentally summoned through 500 consecutive deaths. It exists in a state of permanent instability, tearing through the fabric of the game world.

> **THE DEV — Crown Jewel of the Roster**
> To unlock The Dev, one must master every other character in existence. It is said that this golden entity watches over all gameplay, judging from on high.

## Unlock Progress

To unlock all characters, players must:

1. Master the five default characters
2. Accumulate **225,000 PTS** for shop purchases
3. Achieve eating milestones from 20 to 100+ food
4. Complete speedrun challenges under 45-90 seconds
5. Conquer all game modes and reach high leaderboard positions
6. Discover secret Easter eggs and hidden URLs
7. Play during specific seasonal events
8. Devote **100+ games** to unlock all variants

Only the most dedicated players will ever see **THE DEV**—the ultimate crown jewel of Serpentine's character roster.
