## Standard Mode

*The classic snake experience — pure, refined, timeless.*

### What Is It?

Standard Mode is the foundational gameplay of Serpentine. Guide your snake across the grid, consume energy pellets to grow longer, and avoid collisions with walls and your own tail. Survival is the name of the game.

### How to Launch

```
Main Menu > GAME MODES > STANDARD > Difficulty Select
```

### How You Win / Lose

- **Win Condition:** Achieve the highest score possible before the inevitable end. There is no victory screen — only your final score.
- **Lose Condition:** Collide with a wall or your own body. One mistake ends the run.

### Unique Mechanics

- **Grid Movement:** Your snake moves in discrete steps across the game grid.
- **Growth System:** Each food pellet consumed adds one segment to your tail.
- **Speed Scaling:** As your snake grows longer, the game may incrementally increase speed (depending on difficulty).
- **No Power-ups:** This mode strips away all abilities — pure skill and awareness.

### Scoring System

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Consume food pellet | +10 points base |
| Combo bonus | +2 points per consecutive pickup |
| No-collision streak | Score multiplier increases over time |

### Difficulty Levels

| Level | Grid Speed | Speed Increase | Wall Collision |
|-------|------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Easy** | Slow | Minimal | Enabled |
| **Normal** | Moderate | Gradual | Enabled |
| **Hard** | Fast | Aggressive | Enabled |
| **Nightmare** | Very Fast | Steep | Enabled |

### Tips & Strategies

- **Plan Ahead:** Always keep mental track of your tail's position. As you grow, maneuvering space decreases.
- **Corner Control:** The corners of the grid are high-risk zones — avoid trapping yourself there.
- **Food Chasing:** Don't chase food erratically. Create safe paths to collect it methodically.
- **Breathing Room:** Maintain open space around your head whenever possible.

## ChronoShift

*Bend time. Rewrite fate. Outlast the paradox.*

### What Is It?

ChronoShift introduces temporal manipulation to snake gameplay. Your Chrono-Meter charges over time, granting access to abilities that rewind, slow, accelerate, or freeze the timeline. Survive waves of increasingly aggressive enemies while managing your temporal energy.

### How to Launch

```
Main Menu > GAME MODES > CHRONOSHIFT > Difficulty Select
```

### How You Win / Lose

- **Win Condition:** Survive as long as possible while racking up points. High scores are achieved through skillful temporal ability usage.
- **Lose Condition:** Take damage from temporal hazards or collide with walls/enemies during critical moments.

### Unique Mechanics

- **Chrono-Meter:** A charging resource that powers all temporal abilities. Must be managed carefully.
- **Rewind (Q):** Rewind the last 3 seconds of gameplay, reversing enemy positions and recovering from mistakes.
- **Slow-Mo (W):** Slow time to 25% speed, giving you extra reaction time to navigate hazards.
- **Fast-Forward (E):** Accelerate time to 200% speed, useful for rushing through safe windows or charging the Chrono-Meter.
- **Temporal Pulse (R):** Freeze all enemies in place for 2 seconds, creating a safe escape window.

| Ability | Chrono Cost | Duration |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Rewind | 30% | Instant (3 sec rewind) |
| Slow-Mo | 20% | 4 seconds |
| Fast-Forward | 15% | 3 seconds |
| Temporal Pulse | 50% | 2 seconds |

### Scoring System

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Survive 10 seconds | +50 points |
| Collect chrono orb | +15 points |
| Use Rewind successfully | +25 points |
| Complete slow-mo kill | +40 points |
| Temporal Pulse multi-kill | +100 points |

### Difficulty Levels

| Level | Enemy Speed | Enemy Count | Chrono Drain |
|-------|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Cadet** | Slow | Low | Gentle |
| **Veteran** | Moderate | Medium | Standard |
| **Elite** | Fast | High | Steep |
| **Paradox** | Very Fast | Extreme | Aggressive |

### Tips & Strategies

- **Meter Management:** Never enter a dangerous zone with a depleted Chrono-Meter. Keep at least 30% in reserve for emergencies.
- **Rewind Discipline:** Save Rewind for genuine mistakes, not panic moments. Practice recognizing when a recovery is impossible.
- **Pulse Timing:** Temporal Pulse is your panic button. Use it when surrounded, not when you have other options.
- **Orb Priority:** Temporal orbs restore Chrono energy — prioritize collecting them in tight situations.

## Sentinel Breach

*Defend the Nexus. Annihilate the swarm.*

### What Is It?

Sentinel Breach is a wave-based defense mode where you protect a central Nexus from waves of hostile sentinels. Your snake becomes a guardian, intercepting and destroying incoming threats before they reach the core.

### How to Launch

```
Main Menu > GAME MODES > SENTINEL BREACH
```

### How You Win / Lose

- **Win Condition:** Survive all waves and achieve the highest score. There is no final victory — endless progression until death.
- **Lose Condition:** The Nexus takes damage from sentinels that breach your defenses. Three breaches end the game.

### Unique Mechanics

- **Nexus System:** A central structure you must protect. It has 3 health points (breaches).
- **Wave Announcements:** Each wave is announced with a countdown, giving you time to prepare.
- **Sentinel Types:** Four distinct enemy archetypes with unique behaviors.

| Sentinel | Behavior | Threat Level |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| **PROBE** | Basic unit, moves in straight lines toward the Nexus | Low |
| **SWARMER** | Fast, spawns in groups, erratic movement patterns | Medium |
| **HUNTER** | Tracks your position, intercepts your path | High |
| **TITAN** | Slow, tanky, splits into multiple PROBEs when destroyed | Critical |

- **Breach Mechanics:** When you destroy a sentinel near your snake, it creates a temporary safe zone. Destroying sentinels prevents them from reaching the Nexus.

### Scoring System

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Destroy PROBE | +10 points |
| Destroy SWARMER | +15 points |
| Destroy HUNTER | +25 points |
| Destroy TITAN | +50 points |
| Complete wave | +200 points |
| Zero-breach wave bonus | +150 points |
| Nexus at full health | Score multiplier x1.5 |

### Difficulty Levels

| Level | Wave Intensity | Sentinel Health | Titan Spawn Rate |
|-------|----------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Training** | Low | Low | Rare |
| **Combat** | Moderate | Standard | Occasional |
| **Assault** | High | High | Frequent |
| **Apocalypse** | Extreme | Very High | Constant |

### Tips & Strategies

- **Prioritize TITANS:** A single Titan can spawn multiple probes. Destroy them before they split.
- **Interceptor Play:** Position yourself between spawn points and the Nexus to intercept incoming threats.
- **Hunter Awareness:** HUNTER units will try to cut you off. Use quick turns or HUNTER's momentum against them.
- **Swarm Control:** SWARMERs are fast but fragile. A well-timed pivot can eliminate entire groups.

## Grid Warfare

*Two serpents enter. One leaves victorious.*

### What Is It?

Grid Warfare transforms Serpentine into a 2-player competitive battle arena. Two snakes face off in rounds of combat — the last snake alive wins the round. First to 5 round wins claims the match.

### How to Launch

```
Main Menu > GAME MODES > GRID WARFARE > 1P/2P > AI Difficulty (if 1P) > Character Select
```

### How You Win / Lose

- **Win Condition (Round):** Be the last snake alive when your opponent collides with a wall, their tail, or your snake.
- **Win Condition (Match):** Win 5 rounds to claim victory.
- **Lose Condition (Round):** Collide with a wall, your own body, or your opponent's body.
- **Lose Condition (Match):** Lose 5 rounds before your opponent.

### Unique Mechanics

- **Round System:** Each round resets the grid. Snake positions and lengths reset to baseline.
- **Head-to-Head Combat:** Your body becomes a hazard. Force your opponent into collision courses.
- **Character Selection:** Each character may have subtle stat variations (speed, acceleration, size modifiers).
- **Power Pellet Spawns:** Periodic power pellets may appear that temporarily increase speed or grant brief invulnerability.

| Round Result | Points |
|--------------|--------|
| Round Win | +1 to round count |
| Match Win (5 rounds) | +500 points |
| Perfect Sweep (5-0) | +1000 bonus points |
| Survival Bonus (longer last life) | +50 points |

### Difficulty Levels (AI Opponent)

| Level | AI Reaction | AI Prediction | Aggression |
|-------|--------------|----------------|------------|
| **Novice** | Slow | Minimal | Low |
| **Adept** | Moderate | Basic | Medium |
| **Veteran** | Fast | Advanced | High |
| **Master** | Very Fast | Expert | Maximum |

### Tips & Strategies

- **Control the Center:** Controlling the middle of the grid limits your opponent's escape routes.
- **Build Walls:** Use your tail to create barriers that force opponents into dangerous positions.
- **Patience Pays:** Don't rush. Let your opponent make the mistake while you maintain safe positioning.
- **Read the Opponent:** Watch for patterns in AI or human movement. Exploit predictability.
- **Exit Routes:** Always know your escape path. Never corner yourself.

## Firewall Breach

*The arena shrinks. The walls close in. How long can you survive?*

### What Is It?

Firewall Breach introduces a shrinking arena mechanic. A deadly firewall contracts toward the center of the grid every few seconds, eating into your playable space. Your only defense is to consume food to temporarily push the firewall back.

### How to Launch

```
Main Menu > GAME MODES > FIREWALL BREACH
```

### How You Win / Lose

- **Win Condition:** Survive as long as possible while the firewall continuously contracts.
- **Lose Condition:** The firewall catches your snake, or you collide with a wall when no safe space remains.

### Unique Mechanics

- **Shrinking Arena:** The playable grid area contracts over time. The firewall is instant death.
- **Firewall Pushback:** Consuming food generates breach points. Accumulated breach points push the firewall back, expanding the safe zone.
- **Food Density:** Food spawns more frequently as the arena shrinks, creating risk-reward scenarios.
- **Speed Escalation:** The firewall contracts faster as the game progresses.

| Mechanic | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Firewall Tick** | Every 10 seconds, the firewall advances inward by 1 cell |
| **Breach Points** | Each food = 10 breach points; 100 points pushes firewall back 1 cell |
| **Critical Threshold** | When safe zone < 20% of original, food spawns double |
| **Survival Bonus** | Every 60 seconds of survival = +100 points |

### Scoring System

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Consume food | +10 points |
| Push firewall back | +25 points |
| 60-second survival | +100 points |
| New high-score breach | +500 points |

### Difficulty Levels

| Level | Firewall Speed | Food Spawn Rate | Breach Requirement |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Warmup** | Slow | Standard | 80 points |
| **Standard** | Moderate | Standard | 100 points |
| **Intense** | Fast | Reduced | 120 points |
| **Inferno** | Very Fast | Reduced | 150 points |

### Tips & Strategies

- **Perimeter Awareness:** The firewall comes from all directions. Always know where the edge is.
- **Food Economy:** Consistently eating food is better than sporadic bursts. Maintain a steady income of breach points.
- **Safe Corridors:** As the arena shrinks, create and maintain clear movement corridors.
- **Risk Calculation:** Sometimes it's worth diving toward food in a dangerous zone. Calculate the risk vs. reward.

## Neural Fit

*Match the pattern. Sharpen your reflexes. Unlock your potential.*

### What Is It?

Neural Fit is a shape-matching reflex puzzle mode. Archetype patterns fall from the top of the screen, and you must match them by guiding your snake into the corresponding configuration. It's a test of speed, accuracy, and pattern recognition.

### How to Launch

```
Main Menu > GAME MODES > NEURAL FIT > Select Drill
```

### How You Win / Lose

- **Win Condition:** Successfully match as many archetype patterns as possible within the drill time limit.
- **Lose Condition:** Three failed matches end the drill immediately.

### Unique Mechanics

- **Archetype System:** Each drill focuses on specific geometric patterns (lines, corners, spirals, zigzags).
- **Falling Patterns:** Target configurations appear at the top and descend slowly. Guide your snake into matching shape.
- **Match Window:** You must complete the match before the pattern exits the bottom of the screen.
- **Drill Categories:** Each drill focuses on different archetype families.

| Drill | Focus | Pattern Types |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| **BASIC** | Fundamental shapes | Lines, corners, L-shapes |
| **INTERMEDIATE** | Complex curves | Spirals, waves, S-curves |
| **ADVANCED** | Precision matching | Tight angles, rapid sequences |
| **MASTER** | Ultimate test | All patterns, maximum speed |

### Scoring System

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Successful match | +50 points |
| Speed bonus (fast match) | +10-30 bonus points |
| Combo multiplier | Consecutive matches increase multiplier (x2, x3, x4) |
| Perfect match (center of target) | +100 points |
| Drill completion | Bonus based on remaining lives and time |

### Difficulty Levels

| Level | Pattern Speed | Pattern Complexity | Combo Window |
|-------|---------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Calibration** | Very Slow | Simple | Long |
| **Training** | Slow | Simple | Standard |
| **Exercise** | Moderate | Mixed | Standard |
| **Stress Test** | Fast | Complex | Short |

### Tips & Strategies

- **Pre-Visualize:** Watch the incoming pattern and mentally plan your route before it appears.
- **Center Aiming:** Aim for the center of the target zone for bonus points and fewer errors.
- **Combo Preservation:** Don't rush and miss. A single miss breaks your combo and costs points.
- **Drill Progression:** Master BASIC before attempting INTERMEDIATE. Each drill builds on the previous skills.

## Getting Started

Not sure which mode to play? Here's a quick guide:

- **New to Serpentine?** Start with **Standard Mode** on Easy to learn the basics.
- **Want a challenge?** Try **Sentinel Breach** or **ChronoShift** for intense action.
- **Competitive spirit?** **Grid Warfare** offers head-to-head local multiplayer.
- **Puzzle lover?** **Neural Fit** tests your reflexes and pattern skills.
- **Last-stand intensity?** **Firewall Breach** offers escalating pressure.
